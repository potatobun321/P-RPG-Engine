GP-PRGV5: HOW TO USE THE LEVEL EDITOR
=====================================

The Level Editor is a powerful in-game tool built directly into the engine, allowing you to design maps visually while the game is running.

HOW TO OPEN THE EDITOR:
-----------------------
1. While in-game, press the Tilde key (`~` or `` ` ``) to open the Developer Console.
2. Type `edit()` and press Enter.
3. The Editor Panel will slide in from the left side of your screen.
4. (Optional) Press `~` again to close the console so you can see the entire screen clearly.

EDITOR CONTROLS:
----------------
- LEFT CLICK: Places the currently selected tile onto the grid.
- RIGHT CLICK: Erases a tile (turns it into 'void' / empty space).
- MOUSE WHEEL: Scroll up or down on the left-side panel to view all available tiles.
- CLICK A TILE NAME: Selects that tile type for painting.

CAMERA MOVEMENT (FREE-CAM):
---------------------------
When the Editor is active, your camera automatically detaches from the player.
- Use W, A, S, D to fly the camera around the map freely.
- Hold SHIFT + Z to toggle Free-Cam on or off manually.

SAVING YOUR MAP:
----------------
Your map will AUTOMATICALLY save when you close the game window. 
However, if you want to force a save while editing:
1. Open the console (`~`).
2. Type `save()` and press Enter.
3. The console will print green text confirming the map has been saved to the `world/maps/` folder.

LOADING A DIFFERENT MAP TO EDIT:
--------------------------------
1. Open the console (`~`).
2. Type `load('map2')` (or any other map name) and press Enter.
3. The game will instantly save your current map and warp you to the new one, allowing you to edit multiple maps in one sitting!
