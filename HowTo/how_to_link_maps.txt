GP-PRGV5: HOW TO LINK MAPS TOGETHER
===================================

The engine features a robust, RPG-style "Link ID" system for transitioning between different maps. This allows you to create completely independent, bi-directional doors between maps without them ever conflicting.

STEP 1: PLACING A DOOR (SCENE TILE)
-----------------------------------
1. Open the Level Editor (`edit()` in the console).
2. Select the `Scene` tile from the left panel.
3. Click anywhere on the map to place the door.
4. A dark popup window will instantly appear in the center of your screen.

STEP 2: THE LINKING MENU
------------------------
When the popup appears, you must configure where this door leads:
1. Target Map: Click on the name of the map you want to warp to (e.g., `map2`).
2. Link ID: Click on a unique identifier for this specific connection (e.g., `Link A`).

STEP 3: COMPLETING THE CONNECTION
---------------------------------
For a door to actually work, the *destination map* must have a corresponding door pointing back!

Example of a flawless connection:
- You are on `map1`. You place a door. Target: `map2`. Link ID: `A`.
- You open the console and type `load('map2')` to travel to the second map.
- You place a door on `map2`. Target: `map1`. Link ID: `A`.

Now, if you step on the door in `map1`, the engine scans `map2` looking for "Link A". It finds it, and teleports you exactly on top of it.

WHY LINK IDs ARE POWERFUL:
--------------------------
Because of Link IDs, you can have multiple doors connecting the exact same maps!
- Door 1: `map1` -> `map2` (Link A)
- Door 2: `map1` -> `map2` (Link B)
- Door 3: `map1` -> `map2` (Link C)

They will perfectly connect to their respective Link A, B, and C doors on `map2` without ever getting confused or causing infinite loops.

TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
If you step on a door and it doesn't work, open the Console (`~`). 
You will see red text saying something like:
`Error: No return tile with Link 'A' found in map2!`
This means you forgot to go to `map2` and build the return door!
